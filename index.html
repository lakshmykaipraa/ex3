Sun

The Sun is a massive, glowing ball of gas, primarily made of hydrogen and helium.
It is the center of our solar system and provides the light and heat necessary for life on Earth.
The Sun generates energy through nuclear fusion, where hydrogen atoms fuse to form helium, releasing
enormous amounts of energy in the process. It has a diameter of about 1.4 million kilometers and is
about 93 million miles away from Earth.
